Havermarkt (Oats Market) is a small square in the city of Breda. It is near the Big or Our Lady's Church and the harbor. It is a small square surrounded by cafes with terraces and a famous nightlife spot in Breda. Among others, there is the monumental building the Vogelstruys with asymmetrical gable in 1665.
