In the Molenstraat you see law library, designed by architect
Herman Hertzberger. Because the Molenstraat is quite narrow, the architect
space created by the library to give sloping walls. On the
Instead of the library (on the corner with the Oude Vest) was then located
a gate. Above this gate stood the Lord Caught Huys, where the
most prisoners were housed. The place is cobbled in the
indicated asphalt; The asphalt road is the site of the old city canal.
