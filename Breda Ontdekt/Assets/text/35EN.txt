If you're a beer lover, then Café De Beyerd in Breda is the bar for you. In this pub you can taste 125 different kinds of beer, of which there are even some from its own brewery. Make sure you leave the car at home, or that you run later, because it is difficult to take a beer in this beer-mecca!
