A former convent is a collection of individual houses of beguines, usually in the vicinity of a church and surrounded by a wall with a gate. The court includes Beguine houses, a unique herb garden, a museum, an intimate church, a rectory and a hall. With respect for the past is the Beguine managed in a contemporary way, with the aim to keep this emblematic monument in its original state. The themes of religion, history and cultural heritage play an important role.
