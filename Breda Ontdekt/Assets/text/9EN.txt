The Stadhouder Port is one of the entrances to the Castle of Breda in the center of Breda. Castle features a moat, but the Steward Gate can be reached on a walkway, having first by the Kraanpoort with gatehouse gone. The Stadhouder port is equipped with a triangular pediment and bears the coat of arms of Count William V around which the motto of the Order of the Garter "Honi soit qui mal y pense" (Shame on him who evil thinks). The port's south side was restored in 1959.
