The Chassé Theatre is a large theater in the city of Breda, next to the old Kloosterkazerne. The theater was named after the army officer David Hendrik Chassé. It is housed in a modern building with a striking undulating roof designed by Herman Hertzberger, dating back to 1995. The theater consists of three theaters and three cinemas with a total of 2500 seats.
